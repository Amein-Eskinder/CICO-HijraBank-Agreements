# **CICO CASH MANAGED PLATFORM & OPERATIONS FEES**

## **1\) Managed Model (Scope Snapshot)**

**CICO provides:** fully hosted & managed CICO CASH Digital Wallet & Operator Management Platform, plus day‑to‑day **customer support**, **marketing operations** (technical execution), and **user/operator onboarding**. **All funded from CICO's revenue share allocation.**

**Bank retains:** regulatory compliance & licensing, AML/CFT decisions, product/fee approvals, brand/policy oversight, L3 complaint adjudication, and **marketing budget funding** (via monthly proposal approval). CICO executes operations under Bank oversight per SLA/Scope.

## **2\) One‑Time Fees**

**System Setup & SaaS License (incl. implementation, tenant provisioning, capacity up to 1,000,000 registered users):** **ETB 0.00**.

**Additional License Capacity:** Above 1,000,000 registered users subject to mutual agreement.

**License Warranty:** First **12 months** from Go-Live covers core software defects and bugs (per SLA). This warranty covers defect resolution only, not operational services.

## **3\) NO Monthly Platform Fees**

**There are NO monthly subscription fees, platform access fees, or user-based charges.**

**CICO's sole compensation is derived from:**

1. **50% Revenue Share** on Net Chargeable Transaction Fees (real-time or monthly settlement)
2. **Reimbursement for Bank-approved marketing campaigns** (monthly proposal process)

**CICO funds all platform operations, hosting, infrastructure, support, development, and customizations from its 50% revenue share allocation.**

**The Bank has no obligation to fund operational costs. CICO operates at its own commercial risk.**

## **4\) Services Funded by CICO's Revenue Share**

**CICO funds ALL of the following services from its 50% revenue share allocation:**

* **Hosting & Security:** Ethiopia‑resident hosting, 24/7 monitoring, backups/DR, patching, infrastructure management.

* **Application Maintenance:** bug fixes, patches, enhancements, version upgrades, customizations.

* **Support Operations:** L1/L2/L3 helpdesk (voice/email/chat), incident mgmt., RCA, knowledge base & FAQs.

* **Customer Ops:** onboarding/KYC workflow execution & education, digital registration support.

* **Operator/Agent Ops:** onboarding & training coordination, field playbooks, performance management.

* **Marketing Ops:** campaign setup/execution (technical implementation), CRM segments, in‑app promos, field activation coordination.
  - **Marketing media spend and advertising costs are funded by The Bank** via monthly proposal process (see below).

* **Reporting & Governance:** KPI dashboards, monthly operational reviews, quarterly roadmap updates, monthly marketing performance reports (ROI, CAC, engagement).

## **5\) Revenue Share & Pass‑Through**

**Revenue Sharing on Net Chargeable Transaction Fees:**

* **CICO:** 50%

* **Operator Incentive:** 10% (allocated in real time)

* **Bank:** 40%

**Pass‑Through (deducted first to arrive at Net):** national/payment switch fees; gateway MDR/scheme & dispute fees; SMS/USSD/OTT costs; KYC/AML/biometric verifications; FX/remittance corridor fees; transaction‑linked statutory levies/taxes.

**Ineligible for pass‑through (included in Managed Fee):** core hosting, core application maintenance, and the staff running managed operations.

**Computation:** **Net \= Gross Fees – Pass‑Through** → apply **50/10/40** split on **Net**.

*Example:* Gross ETB 10.00 – Pass‑Through ETB 2.00 \= **Net ETB 8.00** → CICO 4.00, Operator 0.80, Bank 3.20.

## **6\) Changes & Custom Work**

* Minor configuration included in CICO's services (funded from revenue share).

* Standard customizations and enhancements included in CICO's services (subject to resource capacity and prioritization).

* Material custom development requiring significant resources beyond standard service delivery may require separate agreement.

* New third‑party integrations requiring vendor procurement and licensing: costs evaluated via Change Request.

* **All customizations remain CICO's exclusive intellectual property.**

## **7** Payment & Settlement

* **NO Monthly Platform Fees:** There are no subscription fees, platform fees, or user-based charges.

* **Revenue Share:** Real‑time allocation in platform, or **monthly settlement** with statement by the **15th business day** of each month.

* **Marketing Funding:** Bank-funded marketing via monthly proposal process:
  - CICO submits proposal by **5th business day** of each month
  - Bank reviews and approves within **7 business days**
  - Bank pays approved amount within **10 business days** of approval
  - Monthly marketing performance reports provided to Bank

* **Pass‑Through:** Itemized monthly with vendor references; deducted before revenue split.

* **Currency:** ETB to CICO's designated account.

## **8\) Quick Sheet Formulas (for Excel/Sheets)**

* **Net Fees:**  
   `=Gross_Fees - Pass_Through`

* **Revenue Split:**  
   `CICO = Net_Fees*0.50`  
   `Operator = Net_Fees*0.10`  
   `Bank = Net_Fees*0.40`
